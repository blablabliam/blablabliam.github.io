Clear 3D Printed Phone Case

After replacing the battery in my Samsung S7, the rear plate of tempered glass shattered. Rather than buy a new one that would do the same thing to me the next time I try to fix the phone, I modeled the back plate in Onshape and printed a new one out of clear resin in the style of the Clear DS. Currently, the design is in prototype and does not form a perfect seal. It also requires sanding to achieve a fit; I would like to make it possible to print new ones at a moment's notice.
