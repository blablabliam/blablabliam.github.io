Local Volume Database

    The local volume database is an SQL database containing structure, kinematics, distance, chemistry and proper motion data about the dwarf galaxies and globular clusters in the local volume, out to 3 Mpc. The database provides a snapshot of the current research on the local volume, allowing users to test their data and compare it to previous findings. I am working on the database with John Maner, Andrew Pace (A&M), Alex Drilca-Wagner and others at Fermilab.

    This is a poster John and I made to present at the Undergraduate Summer Research Poster Session, August 1st 2018.
