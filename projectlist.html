  • Bioreactor:
    • Made a basic photobioreactor with a few water bottles, a lamp, and an air pump for aquariums.
    • Upgraded to use a sterilized kitty litter tub that holds more than eight liters of water, and built a glass air hose with tubing.
    • Isolated Chlorella from Rock Cut by leaving pond scum in a container for 5 months alternating freezing and room temp without allowing more air to enter. Over time, all non-plant life died, and I was left with Spyrogyra, Diatoms, and Chlorella. Used fractional dillution to separate the species out, and put the Chlorella into the large bioreactor. I have used Miracle Grow and hydroponic fertilizers, and found better results with the hydroponic fertilizer.
    • Project sidelined for the time being since the heating for the bioreactor was not sufficient, and would cost considerable power during the winter.
